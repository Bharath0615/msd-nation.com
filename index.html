Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Following his one-day performance against Sri Lanka, Dhoni replaced Dinesh Karthik in December 2005 as the Indian teams' Test wicket-keeper.[122] Dhoni scored 30 runs in his debut match, that was marred by rain.
